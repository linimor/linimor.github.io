About Me

I am currently a master's student in Computer Science at Harbin Institute of Technology, working with ViLab. My research interests lie in multimodal alignment and fusion, with a growing focus on 3D reconstruction and 3D generation.

Before joining HIT, I received my undergraduate education from University of Shanghai for Science and Technology. I am interested in building methods that connect visual understanding, generative modeling, and geometry, especially systems that can produce reliable 3D representations from diverse input signals.

I am open to research discussions and collaborations. Please feel free to contact me by email at 25S103474@stu.hit.edu.cn.
